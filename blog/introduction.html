## Technology

I've always been a fan of computers and technology. I believe in reinventing the wheel if it helps you understand how the wheel works. That's why I've recently started to deep-dive into compilers and operating systems, and even started building my own compiler (very much unfinished).

Previously, I implemented "Common", an esoteric joke language and my first step into language design and implementation

I've worked on pretty much every domain of software development: frontend, backend, embedded systems, application development, and cybersecurity.
I built an air-gapped network using some old computers and a router I had lying around to experiment with OWASP Juice Shop and other exploitable software on physical hardware.
I'm even managing my own homeserver (which I'm very proud of).

Currently, my obsession lies with my own flight simulator engine thingy.

## TTRPGs

I love playing all kinds of TTRPGs (Tabletop Roleplaying Games). I've played Cyberpunk 2020, DnD, Kids on Bikes, Call of Cthulhu, Vaesen, and Tales from the Loop.

## Engineering

I'm quite fascinated by high-performance engineering, especially modern fighter aircraft and motorsport engineering

### Fighter Jets

Modern fighter jets are crazy complex, high-performance machines, how could you not fall in love with them

For a long time, my favorite jet was the Sukhoi Su-57, solely because of its amazing livery.

Thanks to Wikipedia and Anna Zvereva for taking this amazing picture

Later, I stated that my favorite plane was the Grumman F-14 Tomcat, because its sweeping wing design terrified and fascinated me at the same time, and let's be honest, it looked amazing in the new Top Gun movie.

Thanks to Wikipedia and the US Navy for taking this picture

Nowadays, my favorite aircraft is the McDonnell Douglas F/A-18C Hornet. Its versatility is what got me: being able to land on carriers and carry all sorts of air-to-air and air-to-ground weapons. Coincidentally, it's also the jet I primarily fly in DCS. If you ever see me in DCS, feel free to say hi.

Thanks to Wikipedia and Julian Herzog for taking this really cool picture

### F1

I've been an F1 fan since the 2023 season. My favourite F1 team is, of course, whatever team Lewis Hamilton is at :) currently, that's Ferrari.

However, my main interest in F1 is the design and engineering that goes into the cars. I love studying different design choices across teams, looking at legendary designs from the past and learning what made them so good, and watching technical breakdowns of the newest design changes each team brings to the track.
